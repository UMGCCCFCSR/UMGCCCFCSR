

## Flow Core Shared Resource - University of Maryland Greenebnaum Comprehensive Cancer Center 

The University of Maryland Greenebaum Comprehensive Cancer Center [Flow Cytometry Shared Resource](https://www.medschool.umaryland.edu/cibr/core/umgccc_flow/) (FCSR) offers equipment and technical expertise to the entire campus, as well as outside clients in conducting research in all areas of basic and applied biomedical sciences.

We are an ISAC-recognized [Shared Resource Laboratory](https://isac-net.org/page/Recognized-SRLs#)

![](https://umgccfcss.github.io/Instruments/SRL_Logo.png}

### Current Projects:

- [CytometryInR](https://umgcccfcsr.github.io/CytometryInR/) Our free Cytometry In R mini course, aimed at those with previous cytometry experience, but no to-little coding experience. Being offered both in-person and online, starting February 1st, 2026.
- [InstrumentQC](https://umgccfcss.github.io/InstrumentQC/) Our automated dashboard tracking our instruments QC status, updated daily at 11 AM EST (1600 GMT+0)
- [BISCC_2025](https://github.com/UMGCCFCSS/BISCC_2025) Interactive reference materials for the 2025 Baltimore Introductory Spectral Cytometry Course.
